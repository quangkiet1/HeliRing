# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Biomedical health enthusiasts, biohackers, and design-conscious individuals seeking clinical-grade health tracking (HRV, SpO2, temp) without the bulk or distraction of traditional smartwatches.

## Product Purpose
Promote and showcase the HeliRing Pro, its clinical dual-PPG sensor array, aerospace Titanium shell, and y-sinh metrics, while capturing newsletter signups and user interest.

## Brand Personality
Clinical, Premium, Futuristic, and Minimal.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS-style template layouts, low-contrast text-on-tinted-bg designs, flat illustrations, and aggressive 01/02/03 numbered eyebrows.

## Design Principles
1. **Clinical Credibility**: Health and sensor statistics are presented with scientific authority and absolute legibility.
2. **Material Luxury**: Highlight the premium nature of the Titanium craft through metallic reflections and specular lighting.
3. **Fluid Micro-Animations**: Smooth, interactive 3D and UI transitions that respond immediately to user action, building tactile premium feel.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast values (≥ 4.5:1) for all typography.
- Standard focus rings and keyboard accessibility for buttons and header controls.
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion` to fall back to clean static configurations.
